Community-based home for young men in care

Grounded guidance for young men to grow with confidence.

Oak & Iron is a restorative residential program where teen boys in care build stability, leadership, and lifelong support. We stand beside DSS, schools, and referral partners to deliver measurable outcomes without losing the warmth of a true home.

Program Snapshot

  • Age focus: Young men 12–17
  • Residency: 24/7 therapeutic home
  • Outcomes: Education, wellness, independence

Purpose

Rooted in stability. Built for bold futures.

For residents

We provide a safe, structured home where young men experience consistent care, earn academic credits, and practice leadership in a supportive community.

For partners

We collaborate closely with DSS, grant funders, and school districts to deliver transparent reporting, coordinated plans, and goals that respect every youth's potential.

For the future

Our program equips residents with practical skills—financial literacy, wellness routines, career exploration—so transitions into independence feel achievable.

Residential Program

Structure + support that feels like home.

Daily rhythms

Therapeutic coaching, on-site tutoring, and evening community circles give residents routine and voice.

Whole-person care

Licensed clinicians and mentors integrate mental health, physical wellness, and cultural identity work into personalized plans.

Family partnership

We coordinate with guardians, DSS teams, and schools to keep reunification or independent living milestones within reach.

Guiding values

Confident, not flashy. Realistic & respectful.

  • Strength: Residents are young men with goals—not labels.
  • Community: We cultivate belonging through shared meals, mentorship, and city partnerships.
  • Progress: Every plan includes measurable academic, wellness, and independence benchmarks.
